Sino-Korean School of Multimedia Design


Shanghai University of engineering and technology and East West University of Korea jointly organized the undergraduate education major of art design (Multimedia) (China South Korea cooperation) in 2003. It is also the undergraduate major of the first Sino foreign cooperative multimedia project in China. Students implement the "3 + 1" training mode, that is, they study in Shanghai University of engineering and technology for 3 years and East West University in Korea for 1 year. After completing four years of study, they can obtain various certificates. Since the establishment of the college, foreign teachers have always maintained strong strength, broad international vision and rich practical experience. Every year, the ROK invests more than 20 professional teachers. Shanghai University of engineering and technology of China cooperates with East and West Korea to hold the undergraduate education major of multimedia design. After more than ten years of efforts, it has been recognized by the public and has gradually developed into the cradle of cultivating excellent multimedia designers.
